# Promotion And Prevention

Retrospective preserves reusable learning and prevention follow-up.

## What To Inspect

Inspect the smallest set that explains the work: originating records, acceptance,
findings, evidence, residual risks, worker output and reconciliation notes, changed
files or diffs when implementation shape matters, and repeated questions or
operator corrections that show reusable friction.

Do not rely on chat recall when the records can show what happened.

## Distill The Lesson

Ask what future agents should know, what mistake should not recur, what source or
record should be easier to find, what behavior, strategy, or judgment changed, what
evidence supports or challenges a claim, what follow-up is ticket-ready, and what
risk still needs audit.

Keep one-time execution detail out of durable records unless it explains a future
decision, procedure, or prevention route.

## Route By Shape

Route by shape: knowledge for reusable explanation, procedure, preference,
troubleshooting, atlas, entity context, or retrieval cue; research for investigation
results, rejected paths, tradeoffs, null results, source comparisons, or bounded
conclusions; specs for clarified behavior or interface contract; plans for changed
decomposition, sequencing, rollout, dependency, or recovery strategy; constitution
for durable judgment, principle, constraint, ADR, or roadmap direction; evidence for
observed artifacts or validation output; tickets for executable follow-up; audit for
claims, risks, evidence gaps, implementation shape, or closure stories needing
Ralph-backed challenge.

Promotion identifies the owning surface; it does not bypass that surface's
procedure or authority gate. Before changing specs, plans, constitution, or
research synthesis, use the owning skill and pause for operator authority when the
lesson changes intended behavior, strategy, durable judgment, or conclusions.

## Promotion Quality

A good promotion is findable by likely task words, narrow enough to use without the
whole work thread, honest about observed and unproven claims, linked to source
records or evidence when useful, and placed in one primary surface where possible.

When a lesson touches several surfaces, put the durable claim in the surface that
can maintain it and add lightweight links where useful.

## Prevention Quality

A prevention artifact should reduce a real future failure mode.

Good prevention examples:

- knowledge troubleshooting for a repeated diagnosis path
- knowledge procedure for a repeated manual workflow
- research null result for a tempting but rejected path
- evidence record for a validation observation future agents may need
- spec clarification for ambiguous intended behavior
- follow-up ticket for concrete missing work
- audit record for unresolved risk or unsupported claims

Skip prevention artifacts for ordinary noise. If the lesson is too local, too
uncertain, or already obvious in an easy-to-find record, record no durable
follow-up.
